--- title: Word Cloud in R date: 2016-02-26 tags: ["R", "plot"] ---

With the wordcloud package, an example with fake words the command would look like this:

library(wordcloud)
createWords <- function(w.l = 3) paste(sample(letters, 
    w.l, TRUE), collapse = "")
words = sapply(1:200, function(e) createWords())
freq = c(sample(1:50, 190, T), sample(100:150, 10, 
    T))
freq = freq/sum(freq)
wordcloud(words, freq)

wordcloud(words, freq, colors = sample(c("red", "blue"), 
    length(words), replace = TRUE), ordered.colors = TRUE)
