“Good” ones:

  • eLife is non-profit, open and more modern.
  • PLOS the pioneer in open-access.
  • BMC, although it’s owned by Springer Nature publishing group.
  • F1000 where the manuscript is “published” right away and then transparently peer-reviewed. Once PR, it is indexed in PubMed etc.
  • PeerJ is about open-access and cost-efficient publishing.

“Evil” publishing companies

  • Elsevier.
  • Springer Nature.
